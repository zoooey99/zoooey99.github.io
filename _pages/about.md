Hi there! My name is Zoey Lee. Here are some quick facts that give a good snapshot of my life:

- **I’ve played soccer for 16 years.** I have spent most of my life revolved completely around soccer, including going to boarding schools and living with a host family to pursue my passion. It has always been my dream to play at the collegiate level. I am more than grateful that I was recruited to play at the University of Miami. Not only is it a prestigious university, UM is also in the ACC, the most competitive conference for women’s soccer.

* **I’m pursuing 3 majors and a minor.** My degree declarations are always changing with my evolving interests, but currently I’m pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science (Data Science & Ai track), B.S. in Mathematics (Probability & Statistics track), B.S.C in Immersive Media (Developer track), and a minor in Exercise Physiology. Generally, I hope to work in data science or the XR industry - something athletically related.

- **I Co-Founded and am Co-President of Kappa Theta Pi - UM’s first and only Professional Technology Fraternity.** When I first got to UM in 2022, I searched for an organization centered around tech and community building. To my dismay, I was unable to find what I was looking for. Fortunately, in June 2023, Mia Uy invited me to share her vision of bringing a tech-related professional fraternity to campus. Six months and a lot of work later, we have a board of 10 and a newly initiated class of 23 members! This has been an extremely transformative project in my life and I’m excited to see what more we will accomplish together.

- **I work at the VESL - UMiami’s Virtual Experience Simulation Laboratory - to create XR related products and experiences.** I’ve never had more fun working on technical projects than I’ve had at the VESL. Currently, I am working as the lead 360° Videographer with a concentration in filming UM Athletics. As I progress my skills in Unity, I am hoping to evolve into more of a developing role.

- **I love to read non-fiction books.** There is nothing more satisfying than discovering a new idea and immediately being able to apply it to solve a pain-point in my own life. I live for self-improvement and I also love to engage in discussions on the ideas I read about with friends and loved-ones.


More information about each of these endeavors can be found in their related blog posts. To summarize my life and personality ~ I have gained a hard-working and disciplined mentality from my experience as an athlete. This mentality in combination with my ambition has propelled me into new ventures and projects in my life - namely Kappa Theta Pi and the VESL. Honesty is my guiding core value. I try to be as honest as possible in my thoughts, speech, and actions in order to live an ordered and effective lifestyle. I value community over everything. I strongly believe that my success is measured on the relationships I build rather than the things I create or the status of my career. 

Recently, I have discovered that “an idea is only is good as its packaging,” or as I interpret it, an idea is worthless in the eyes of others if you are unable to communicate it effectively. In addition to practicing my web development skills, I have built this website in an attempt to “package” my life, personality, and accomplishments in a more effective manner. I hope that it serves its purpose and you are able to find some use out of it!

Please feel free to contact me with any questions or concerns - or if even you just want to discuss something interesting :\)

Sincerely,

Zoey Lee
